Help center

Quick guidance for ratings, bookmarks, and deals.

Browse businesses

Rate a business

  • Open a business detail page from the Businesses list.
  • Choose a rating from 1-5 and add a short comment if you want.
  • Submit your review (email verification is required).

Edit or remove your review

  • Find your review on the business detail page.
  • Use the "Edit review" link to update your rating or comment.
  • Changes show up right away after saving.

Use bookmarks

  • Click the ribbon on a business page to add or remove a bookmark.
  • Visit Bookmarks in the navigation to see your saved businesses.
  • Select “View business” to jump back to details quickly.

Download bookmark reports

  • Open your Bookmarks page.
  • Choose "Download PDF" to export your saved list.
  • Use the report for offline notes or sharing.

Search and filter businesses

  • Use the search bar to find a business by name or location.
  • Filter by type to narrow the list.
  • Sort by rating or name for faster browsing.

Redeem deals

  • Open a business detail page and find the Deals section.
  • Press "Redeem deal" to claim an offer.
  • Redeemed deals show as completed on the page.

Need more help?

If something does not look right, log out and back in, then try again. You can also reach out to the site admin for support.
