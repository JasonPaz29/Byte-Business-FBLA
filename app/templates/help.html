Support

Help center

Use this page as the quick reference for reviews, bookmarks, business claims, and deals.

Browse businesses

Rate a business

  • Open a business detail page from the directory.
  • Select a rating from 1 to 5 and add a comment if needed.
  • Submit the review once your email is verified.

Edit or remove a review

  • Find your review on the business detail page.
  • Use the edit action to change the rating or comment.
  • Use delete if the review should be removed entirely.

Use bookmarks

  • Bookmark a business from the business detail page.
  • Open Bookmarks from the navigation to revisit saved places.
  • Export your bookmark list to PDF when needed.

Claim a business

  • Submit a business claim from the businesses page.
  • Track approval status from your request history.
  • Admins can approve or reject with notes.

Search and filter

  • Use search to find names, locations, or categories.
  • Sort by rating or name to scan faster.
  • Filter by category to narrow down the grid.

Redeem deals

  • Open the business detail page and locate the deals section.
  • Redeem the offer directly from the available deal card.
  • Redeemed deals are marked so they are easy to track.

Still blocked?

If something behaves incorrectly, sign out, sign back in, and retry the flow. If it still fails, contact the site administrator.
